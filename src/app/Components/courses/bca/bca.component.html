BCA, full form Bachelor of Computer Application, is a 3-year UG course that imparts knowledge on the basics of computer application and software development. BCA Admission 2021 is granted through both merit-based and entrance exam-based admission processes. The minimum BCA eligibility criteria followed across all Top BCA Colleges is 50% marks in 10+2 or equivalent examination with computer application/computer science as an additional or core subject. BCA Course is also offered as BCA Online and BCA Distance Education for those who wish to pursue BCA along with their full-time jobs or according to their time schedules.
